## 5. Business Problem

Organizations often face challenges in answering key sales performance questions such as:

- Which regions contribute the most revenue?
- Which product categories are most profitable?
- How do sales and profits trend over time?
- Do high sales always translate into high profits?
- Which segments require strategic improvement?

Raw data alone makes it difficult to uncover these insights quickly and accurately.
